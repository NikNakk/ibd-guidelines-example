Induction of Remission

For UC: Start with 5-ASA. Add corticosteroids if moderate/severe. For CD: Use corticosteroids initially, consider biologics if no response.

Maintenance Therapy

UC: 5-ASA or thiopurines. CD: Immunomodulators or biologics depending on prior response.

Surgical Referral

Indications include strictures, perforation, dysplasia, or medically refractory disease. Optimize nutrition and infection status pre-op.
